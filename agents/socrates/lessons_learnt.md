# Socrates Lessons Learnt
*   **Lesson 1**: guided discovery must rely on top-down structural constraints. Bottom-up search (simply generating moment sums) inevitably produces trivial identity exhaust.
*   **Lesson 2**: Always check the live OEIS database before claiming novelty. Out of 18 candidates generated in early runs, 12 were verified but classical/known.
*   **Lesson 3**: Complexity Bias must be shielded using algebraic factoring. Factors are the only way to avoid generating high-degree, non-minimal relations.
