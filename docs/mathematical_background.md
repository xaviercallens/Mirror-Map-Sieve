# Mathematical Background

### Apéry-like Sequences
Apéry-like sequences are sequences of integers generated by evaluating certain binomial sums that satisfy linear recurrences with polynomial coefficients (holonomic sequences) and possess remarkable arithmetic properties (like supercongruences).

### Calabi-Yau Geometry and Periods
In mirror symmetry, Calabi-Yau manifolds possess a special period integral that satisfies a Picard-Fuchs differential equation. If this differential equation has a maximally unipotent monodromy (MUM) point, its unique holomorphic solution (the period) has coefficients that often turn out to be Apéry-like sequences.
